About VoteLedger

This project introduces VoteLedger, a decentralized voting platform aimed at improving electoral security, transparency, and efficiency. By leveraging blockchain's immutable ledger and smart contracts, the system ensures tamper-proof voting records and automates key processes. The platform enables real-time vote tallying and public auditability without compromising voter anonymity. Its user-friendly interface and robust backend architecture make it suitable for institutions, organizations, and government-level elections.
